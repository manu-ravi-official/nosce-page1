WELCOME TO NOSCE

To be successful in this increasingly competitive world, one should be well aware about oneself. Self awareness is all about knowing your emotions, personal strengths & weaknesses and having a sense of self worth. NOSCE is a Personal Enrichment & Support Team. We have a team of self motivated and professionally qualified members to support you in your pursuit of knowing yourself. We ought to nourish your talents and build your confidence so that you can face the day today challenges successfully. Ongoing placement assistance and technical aptitude training would be an added advantage. Also, we work with corporates & provide customized training to employees based on their projected needs. We are blessed with a wide range of satisfied customers, both from educational and corporate sectors whose feedback and support drives us forward. Hoping to serve you more & better.
